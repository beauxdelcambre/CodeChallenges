Squid Game: The #1 Show on Netflix


This article was written on October 25, 2021

Squid Game is now offically the most watched series ever on Netflix!

Squid Game, which started streaming exclusively on Netflix on Sept. 17
has beome the most watched Netflix series in the world. The show focuses on
a desperately indebted group of people in South Korea. They're first tricked
into a tournament of children's games with deadly consequences that are shocking
the world, so, with nothing left to lose, let the games begin.......

Watch the trailer now!


Episode List & Descriptive text:

E01. Red Light, Green Light
Hoping to win easy money, a broke and desperate
Gi-hun agrees to take part in an enigmatic game.
Not long into the first round, unforeseen horrors unfold.

E02. Hell
Split on whether to continue or quit, the group
holds a vote. But their realities in the outside world
may prove to be just as unforgiving as the game.

E03. The Man with the Umbrella
A few players enter the next round - which promises
equal doses of sweet and deadly - with hidden
advantages. Meanwhile, Jun-ho sneaks his way inside.

E04. Stick to the Team
As alliances form among the players, no one is safe
in the dorm after lights-out. The third game challenges
Gi-hun's team to think strategically.

E05. A Fair World
Gi-hun and his team take turns keeping guard
through the night. The masked men encounter trouble
with their co-conspirators.

E06. Gganbu
Players pair off for the fourth game. Gi-hun grapples
with a moral dilemma, Sang-woo chooses self-preservation
and Sae-byeok shares her untold story.

EO7. VIPS
The Masked Leader welcomes VIP guests to the
facility for a front-row viewing of the show. In the
fifth game, some players crack under pressure.

E08. Front Man
Ahead of the last round, distrust and disgust
run deep among the finalists. Jun-ho makes a getaway,
determined to expose the game's dirty secrets.

E09. One Lucky Day
The final round presents another cruel test but
this time, how it ends depends on just one player.
The game's creator steps out of the shadows.

“Life is like a game, there are many players.
If you don’t play with them, they’ll play with you.”
